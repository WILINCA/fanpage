He had the chance to meet many professional snooker players, including his personal favourite, the one of the greatest players of all time, Ronald Antonio "Ronnie" O'Sullivan.

In snooker, the maximum break under normal circumstances is 147. Ronnie O'Sullivan holds the record of thirteen competitive maximum breaks, and he also has the record for the fastest competitive maximum break at 5 minutes 20 seconds, set at the 1997 World Championship.
